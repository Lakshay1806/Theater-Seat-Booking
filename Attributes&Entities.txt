1️⃣ Entities & Attributes
🎬 1. Theater
Attributes:
theater_id (Primary Key)
theater_name
location
total_screens
🎥 2. Screen
Attributes:
screen_id (Primary Key)
theater_id (Foreign Key)
screen_name
total_seats
💺 3. Seat
Attributes:
seat_id (Primary Key)
screen_id (Foreign Key)
row_number
seat_number
seat_type (Normal / Premium / VIP)
price
🎞 4. Movie
Attributes:
movie_id (Primary Key)
movie_name
duration
genre
language
⏰ 5. Show
Attributes:
show_id (Primary Key)
movie_id (Foreign Key)
screen_id (Foreign Key)
show_date
show_time
👤 6. Customer
Attributes:
customer_id (Primary Key)
name
email
phone_number
🎟 7. Booking
Attributes:
booking_id (Primary Key)
customer_id (Foreign Key)
show_id (Foreign Key)
booking_date
total_amount
payment_status
🪑 8. Booking_Seat (Weak/Bridge Entity)
(Used for many-to-many relationship between Booking and Seat)
Attributes:
booking_seat_id (Primary Key)
booking_id (Foreign Key)
seat_id (Foreign Key)
